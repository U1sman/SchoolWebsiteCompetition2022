JAFFAR PUBLIC SCHOOL

ABOUT US

Overview

Jaffar Public School is an independent, co-educational, English Medium school in Karachi, Pakistan.
Jaffar Public School was inaugurated in 1989 by Mrs. Yasmeen Jaffar. The school initially offered education for Ordinary
Level based on the British GCSE system only until the 8th grade. However, later, O Level studies from the 9th to 11th grade were also offered.

Vision

We value the culture of unity, faith and discipline. We want our students to enrich themselves with this culture along with respect for each other and high moral values to meet the challenges of the real world.
