Sports Day 2023

Sports day is a highly anticipated event that takes place every year in our school.
It's a day filled with excitement, friendly competition, and camaraderie among students. Our sports
day is no exception! During sports day, students get the opportunity to participate in a variety of
activities, ranging from traditional track and field events to unique and creative games. These can
include running races, relay races, long jump, high jump, tug-of-war, and even water balloon tosses.
What's great about sports day is that it's not just about winning, but about having fun and showing
good sportsmanship. Everyone can participate, and each student's effort is recognized and celebrated.
Whether you're an athlete or not, sports day is a chance to get active, make friends, and enjoy a day
of fun with your classmates.

Magnifi-Science

Magnifiscience is a wonderful place for students to explore and learn
about science in a fun and interactive way. At Magnifiscience, students can get hands-on
experience with a range of exhibits that showcase various scientific concepts. They can
learn about everything from the human body to the natural world, and even outer space.
One of the highlights to Magnifiscience is the chance to participate in interactive
demonstrations and workshops. These activities are designed to engage students in
learning and make scientific concepts more accessible and understandable.
Magnifiscience will undoubtedly inspire and encourage students to pursue their
interests in science. It's an excellent opportunity to ignite a passion for
learning and discovery, which can lead to exciting career paths and lifelong interests.

Bake Sale

Bake Sale is a delicious and exciting event that brings students together to share their
culinary skills and creativity. Students can set up stalls and sell a variety of food items, ranging from
homemade snacks to full meals. This is an opportunity for students to showcase their cooking skills, creativity,
and cultural diversity. Students can work together in groups to prepare their stalls and plan their menu.
They can also experiment with new recipes, incorporate unique ingredients, and create innovative dishes
that reflect their cultural background or personal tastes. This day is not only a chance for students
to showcase their culinary skills but also to promote healthy eating and sustainable food practices.
They can use their stalls to raise awareness about issues related to food waste, food insecurity,
and the environmental impact of food production. It is also an opportunity for students to interact
with their peers and community members in a fun and engaging way. They can share their love of food
and culture with others and learn about other students' food traditions and preferences.
